Tufts Gaming Hub is here for any Tufts undergrads, grad students, or even faculty, along with friends from home, other colleges, or really anywhere else.

We have competitive teams for 8 different games this year, including but not limited to: League of Legends, Smash Bros. Ultimate, and Valorant. We have a huge community of casual players for all sorts of games. We also host fun events like game nights regularly.
